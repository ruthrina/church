Presbytery Proposal

The Presbytery is an essential space for the priests of our parish to live, reflect, and prepare for their sacred duties. This proposal outlines the plan for building a comfortable and modern residence for our priests. Below are the images of the proposed house and key details about the design.

Images of the Proposed House for Priests

Presbytery Project Overview

This house will serve as a peaceful home for the priests. It includes spacious bedrooms, a common living area, a dining hall, and an office space for spiritual preparation. The design is inspired by simplicity and modern comfort, reflecting the serene and sacred life of the clergy.
