Funeral Sacrament

The Funeral Sacrament is a solemn and sacred moment where the parish comes together with families to bid farewell to their loved ones. It is a time to remember, pray, and celebrate the life of the departed, as we believe in the promise of eternal life through Christ.

Funeral Arrangements

At St. Cyprian Kawanda Parish, we offer support and assistance to families planning funeral services. Our clergy and staff work closely with you to ensure a dignified and respectful ceremony, honoring the life and faith of the deceased.

Please contact our parish office to arrange for funeral services and to discuss the necessary preparations and available dates.
