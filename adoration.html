Special Adoration Events

Additional adoration times may be scheduled during Lent and Advent. Please check our website for updates.

Guidelines for Adoration

  • Arrive 10 minutes early to prepare for prayer.
  • Maintain a reverent silence during adoration.
  • All are welcome to spend time in prayer and reflection.
