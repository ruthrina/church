Our Sub Parishes

Explore the six sub-parishes under St. Cyprian Kawanda

St. Dominic

St. Dominic Sub Parish is renowned for its community outreach, fostering spiritual growth, and providing support to the youth in Kawanda.

St. Charles

Known for its vibrant church choir and active participation in local events, St. Charles Sub Parish is at the heart of spiritual and social development in the area.

St. Andrew

St. Andrew Sub Parish is focused on prayer, spiritual retreats, and missions, nurturing a devout and prayerful community of believers.

St. Kizito

St. Kizito Sub Parish offers robust programs for youth and children, along with charitable activities aimed at uplifting the less privileged in the Kawanda community.

St. Henry

St. Henry Sub Parish focuses on family values and social justice, providing spiritual guidance and community support to families in need.

St. Noah

With a focus on leadership development and spiritual growth, St. Noah Sub Parish encourages active participation in church life and community services.
