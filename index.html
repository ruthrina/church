Welcome to our parish

It is my pleasure to welcome you to the website for the parish of St. Cyprian Catholic Church. Whether you are a new-comer to our area in search of a parish home, a member of the parish family or your life of faith has drawn you to seek this information. It is my conviction that God has a plan for you to become involved in the worship and ministries of the church.

I invite you to review our website in its infancy and encourage you to contact us if you have questions about our parish or its ministries. If you are a parish member, there is a wealth of information for you to use and to offer your many talents in service to your parish.

Father Galiwango Robert, Parish Priest

Volunteer With Us.

We invite you to make a difference by volunteering at St Cyprian. Your unique skills and passion can transform lives and strengthen our community. Whether you help at events, support our outreach, or join specific ministries, there's a role for everyone. Volunteering not only benefits those in need but also creates lasting friendships and memories. Come and experience the joy of giving back. Everyone is welcome, no matter your experience. Join us in spreading love and kindness through your time and talents!

I'm Interested

Join a Connect Group.

Connect groups offer a wonderful opportunity to grow in your faith while building meaningful relationships. In these small groups, you can share your thoughts and experiences in a supportive environment. Engage in enriching discussions, enjoy group activities, and explore your beliefs with others on similar journeys. These gatherings are open to everyone, regardless of where you are in your spiritual path. Join us to deepen your faith and forge lasting friendships. Discover the joy of community and connection at Hesed!
