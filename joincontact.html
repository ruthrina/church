Join a Connect Group

Experience community and grow in faith together!

What is a Connect Group?

Connect Groups are small gatherings of parish members who come together to grow in faith, support one another, and foster deeper relationships within our community. Whether you are new to the parish or have been here for years, joining a Connect Group is a great way to get involved!

These groups meet regularly to discuss scripture, pray together, and share life experiences. They provide a safe and welcoming environment for everyone to connect and grow in their faith journey.

Benefits of Joining a Connect Group

  • Build Lasting Relationships: Connect with fellow parishioners who share your faith and values.
  • Grow Spiritually: Engage in meaningful discussions and deepen your understanding of scripture.
  • Support One Another: Share joys and challenges, and pray for each other.
  • Participate in Service Projects: Work together on outreach and community service initiatives.

How to Join a Connect Group
