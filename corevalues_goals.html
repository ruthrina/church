Objectives

Sanctification of the Souls

To guide and nurture spiritual growth through sacraments, prayer, and pastoral care for the eternal salvation of all souls.

Excellence Performance

To maintain the highest standards in liturgical celebrations, pastoral services, and administrative functions.

Strong Team Collaboration

To foster unity and effective cooperation among clergy, staff, and parishioners in serving the church's mission.

Set and Accomplish Strategic Goals

To develop and implement clear, achievable objectives that advance our parish's vision and mission.

Deliver Exceptional Community Satisfaction

To ensure our parish services and programs meet and exceed the spiritual and social needs of our community.

Build a Positive and Inclusive Culture

To create a welcoming environment where all members feel valued, respected, and encouraged to participate.

Optimize Efficiency and Resource Management

To responsibly steward our parish resources, ensuring effective utilization for maximum impact.

Develop Strong Leadership and Accountability

To cultivate capable leaders and maintain transparent governance at all levels of parish administration.

Measure and Analyze Performance

To regularly evaluate and improve our programs, services, and initiatives through data-driven assessment.

Accountability to Stakeholders

To maintain transparent communication and responsible stewardship with all parish stakeholders.
