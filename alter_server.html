Altar Servers

Introduction

Altar servers play a vital role in the liturgical celebrations of the Church. They assist the priests during Mass and other sacraments, helping to create a reverent and prayerful atmosphere.

Role of Altar Servers

As an altar server, you will participate actively in the liturgy by carrying out various duties, such as:

  • Carrying the cross and candles during the entrance and exit procession.
  • Assisting the priest during the celebration of the Eucharist.
  • Preparing the altar and assisting with the placement of liturgical items.
  • Helping to maintain the sacred environment during the service.

Responsibilities

Altar servers are expected to:

  • Arrive on time for Mass and other liturgical events.
  • Dress appropriately in designated altar server attire.
  • Follow instructions from the priest and lead server.
  • Participate actively and reverently in all liturgical activities.

How to Become an Altar Server

If you are interested in becoming an altar server, please speak with the priest or the lead altar server after Mass. Training sessions will be provided to equip you with the necessary skills and knowledge.
